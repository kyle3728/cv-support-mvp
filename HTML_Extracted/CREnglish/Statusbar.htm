Statusbar

Status

On the left side of the statusbar is the status of the current or last process.

Play Speed

Walk Speed allows you to control, in real time, the speed of the Tour when Played.

Exposure

Brightness controls the background brightness of the Scene.

 

You can double click on the left (brighten) and right (dim) mouse button to control the brightness of the model.

Ambient

Ambient controls the brightness of the Ambient (Sun) light.
