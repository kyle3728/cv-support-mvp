Right Click Roll Out Tray Section View - Section Case

 

Following are the possible options when right clicking on a Roll Out Tray in the Section Case view:

  •  Delete - Deletes the selected Roll Out Tray.

  •  Properties - Following are the possible options on the Roll Out Tray Tab:

    • Construction - Allows you to select the Construction Method to use for the selected Roll Out Tray.

    • Material - Allows you to select the Roll Out Material Schedule to use for the selected Roll Out Tray.

    • Change Guide Type - Will bring up the Drawer Guide selector and allow you to change the Drawer Guide for the selected Roll Out Tray.

    • Edit Drawer Box - Allows you to Section Edit the selected Roll Out. See the Drawer Section Editor area of the Right Click Drawer topic.

    • Open Amount Slider - Will allow you to open the selected Roll Out Tray to a maximum distance as specified by the Length of the Drawer Guide the Roll Out Tray is using. Opening amount is specified for the Drawer Guide in the Material Catalog.
