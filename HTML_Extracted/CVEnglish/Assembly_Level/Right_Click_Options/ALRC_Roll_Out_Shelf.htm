Right Click Roll Out Shelf Section View - Section Interior

 

Following are the options when right clicking on a Roll Out Shelf in the Section Case view:

  •  Delete - Deletes the selected Roll Out Shelf.

  •  Properties - There are two options on the Roll Out Shelf Tab:

    • Change - Will bring up the Drawer Guide selector and allow you to change the Drawer Guide for the selected Drawer.

    • Open Amount Slider - Will allow you to open the selected Roll Out Shelf to a maximum distance as specified by the Length of the Drawer Guide the Roll Out Shelf is using. Opening amount is specified for the Drawer Guide in the Material Catalog.
