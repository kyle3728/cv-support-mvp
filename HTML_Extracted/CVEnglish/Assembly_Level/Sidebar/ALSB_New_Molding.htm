New Molding -

To add Molding at the Assembly Level, click the New Molding button in the sidebar.

In the New Molding window, select the desired Style and Profile in the pull down menus. Click OK to continue.

The Material displayed is controlled by the active Molding Material Schedule. You can change to an alternate Material by clicking the Change button. To revert back to the Material Schedule default Material simply select the Reset button.

Use the Molding Editor to add and edit points of the new Molding. See the Shape Editor topic form more information.
