Flutes - (Face Frame and Frame Overlay Style)

The Flutes Tab at Cabinet Properties has two additional options at the Assembly Level:

  • Flute Left Stile - This applies the Flute Specification entered here to the Left Stile of the active Cabinet.

  • Flute Right Stile - This applies the Flute Specification entered here to the Right Stile of the active Cabinet.

See the Job Level Flutes topic for more information.

 

Remember changes made here will only affect the current Assembly. Also, changes made here are attached as permanent (unless you remove them) overrides on the Assembly; which means that if you Save this Assembly into a Catalog these overrides will also be Saved.
