Alias

This option will add an Alias for your selected Material. Aliases are Material which get most of their Properties from the Parent Material with a few Properties that are unique (differentiators). This allows you to do things like create Panel Stock which would be the same with the exception of their Name, Sheet Size, and Cost. Most Material Types have special Properties used as differentiators.

Simply type the unique Name and Description for your Alias.

The Alias will be available as a sub-item to its parent Material.

Certain fields will be available for editing (differentiators) while other will be locked indicating they are derived from the parent Material.
