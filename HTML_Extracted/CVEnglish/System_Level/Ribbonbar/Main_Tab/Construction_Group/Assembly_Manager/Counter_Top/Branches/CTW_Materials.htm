Materials

The following diagram shows the options available in the Materials object. Click a specific option in the diagram for more information or continue through this topic for information on all the Material options.

Surface & Substrate (Standard #1)

There are two options for Surface & Substrate:

  • Surface Only - Will only use the Part from your Counter Top Material Schedule outlined in red below to define the Top (Counter Top Surface).

  • Surface & Substrate - Will use the two Parts from your Counter Top Material Schedule outlined in red below to define the Top (Counter Top Surface & Rough Top).

Build Up (Standard #2)

Do you include build up with this Construction Method?

Edge Cap Location (Standard #36)

There are two options for Edge Cap Location:

  • Position on Edge

  • Position under Top

Edge Cap Width (Standard #3)

Enter the Width of the Edge Cap for this Top Type.

Splash Height (Standard #40)

Specify the Splash Height or No Splash here.

Splash Surface & Substrate (Standard #43)

There are two options for Surface & Substrate:

  • Surface Only - Will only use the Part from your Counter Top Material Schedule outlined in red below to define the Top (Splash).

  • Surface & Substrate - Will use the two Parts from your Counter Top Material Schedule outlined in red below to define the Splash (Splash & Rough Splash).

One or Two Piece Splash (Standard #41)

There are two options for One or Two Piece Splash:

  • One Piece

  • Two Piece - Specifies a two piece Splash and asks for the from the Wall for the front of the Splash.

Two Piece Splash Join Type (Standard #42)

There are three options for Two Splash Joint Type:

  • Miter

  • Butt Joint - 1st Piece Long

  • Butt Joint 2nd Piece Long
