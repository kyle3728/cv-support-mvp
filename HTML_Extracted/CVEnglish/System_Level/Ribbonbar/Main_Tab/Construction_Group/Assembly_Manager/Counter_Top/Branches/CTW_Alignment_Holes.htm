Alignment Holes

The following diagram shows the options available in the Alignment Holes object. Click a specific option in the diagram for more information or continue through this topic for information on all the Alignment Hole options.

Alignment Holes (Standard #9)

Would you like your CNC machine to mill alignment holes to assist in positioning build up?

You have two options for Alignment Holes:

  • No - Don't Mill Alignment Holes

  • Yes - Mill Alignment Holes

Alignment Hole Offset (Standard #10)

Alignment holes will be placed in the corners of the build up. Enter the offset from the corner of the build up to the center of the hole.

Alignment Hole Diameter (Standard #11)

Enter the diameter of the alignment hole.

Alignment Hole Depth (Standard #12)

Enter the depth the alignment hole is to be bored into the back side of the top surface or substrate.

Front Alignment Holes (Standard #13)

Would you like to have alignment holes on the outside of the build up as well as the inside corners.

Maximum Alignment Hole Span (Standard #14)

Enter the maximum space you will allow between two holes before adding additional holes.
