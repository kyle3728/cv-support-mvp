Shoe Section

This option sets Shoe Section options, it sets the sizing as well as adjacent part joinery options. The Shoe Section object is automatically joined together using egg-crate style joinery.

 Regardless of how you answer these questions, you will not see the Shoe Section in a Closet or Cabinet until you edit a Closet or Cabinet and place one there, and save the modified Closet or Cabinet back to your library.
