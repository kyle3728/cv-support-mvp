Nailer

Whether you have a top nailer, mid nailer, or a bottom nailer is set here. Whether you choose to have them outside or inside of your back is also set. The width of a nailer is entered in number format.

The following diagram shows the options available in the Nailer Branch. Click a specific option in the diagram for more information or continue through this topic for information on all the options.
