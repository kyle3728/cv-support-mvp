Line Boring

This option sets Line Boring options, it sets the dimensions, type, and position conditions.

Line Boring is typically used for Adjustable Shelf, and Hardware Mounting (Hinges, Drawer Guides, Wire Baskets, and Hanging Section).

One additional consideration regarding Hardware location. If you choose to use (32mm) System Holes for location of your Hardware, the information you have setup in the appropriate area may be effected. I.E., if you choose to mount Drawer Guides in system holes, your Drawer Boxes will be sized accordingly. Additionally, you may choose to use the option to split Drawer Faces and Doors on system holes if you choose to mount on system holes.
