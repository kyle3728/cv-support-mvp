Cabinet Flutes

Select Regular for Flutes of an even length, or Diamond for Flutes that are longer in the center of the Stile. Configure the dimensions in the data boxes for placement of the Flutes. This feature works on Left and Right Stiles in Face Frame Construction. The Flutes are applied via the Flutes Tab in Assembly Properties in the Section Editor or to other Parts via Right Click Part Ortho Properties and are machine (CNC) ready.
