Cross Section -

Ribbonbar

There is a special ribbonbar area when in Cross Section mode.

These options will allow you to work with any Saved Sections you have created via the Cross Section Right Click Save Section option.

You can either use the drop down menu to select or use the Previous Saved and Next Saved options to scroll through your Saved Sections.

The Edit Saved option allows you to Delete any Saved Sections you no longer need in the Job.

The Lock Position option keeps you from accidentally changing your view. Disable this option if you want to change from the Saved Section. Note: Changing the Section with this option disabled does not re-save the Section. You would need to use Save Section to save a new orientation.

You can also add CAD to Saved Sections.

In Plan View

The Cross Section option allows you to display Cross Section views of your Drawing.

There are three main areas in the Cross Section sidebar:

Screen Area

The screen area allows you to set your cut line (red line) for your cross section as well as viewing direction. In Vertical Elevation mode there are several available options:

  • This button will rotate the cut line (red line) 90 degrees to obtain a different cut for your cross section.

  • These buttons control which direction you are looking at the cut line (red line) from.

Elevations

There are two viewing choices available here:

  1. Vertical - Allows you to make vertical Cross Sections of your Plan view.

OR

  1. Horizontal - Allows you to make horizontal Cross Sections of your Plan view.

Hide

This area allows you to hide certain components in your Cross Section views.

In Elevation View

The Cross Section option allows you to display Cross Section views of your Drawing.

 

There are four main areas in the Elevation Cross Section sidebar:

Screen Area

The screen area allows you to set your cut line (red line) for your cross section as well as viewing direction. In Vertical Elevation mode there are several available options:

These buttons control which direction you are looking at the cut line (red line) from.

Wall # Face

There are three viewing choices available here:

  1. Front - Selecting this option will only display Objects placed on the Front Face of the active Wall.

  2. Back - Selecting this option will only display Objects placed on the Back Face of the active Wall.

  3. Both - Selecting this option will display Objects placed on both the Front and Back Face of the active Wall.

Elevations

There are two viewing choices available here:

  1. End - Allows you to make End Cross Sections of your Elevation view.

  1. Wall Face - Allows you to make a Wall Face Cross Sections of your Elevation view.

Hide

This area allows you to hide certain components in your Cross Section views.
