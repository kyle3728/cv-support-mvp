Core Bid Center

The CABINET VISION Core comes with a basic Bid Center. It has a predefined Report as shown in the image below. You can control the content of this Report by choosing the Options button (see below for more information) in the ribbonbar. You can also Export the Report to an RTF (Rich Text Format) file for editing in your Favorite Word processor.

Options

There are five tabs in the Options (Bid Setup) area:

Item Totals

There are two main areas on the Item Totals tab:

    • Show the Total Price For These Materials - Simply select any Materials whose Price you want to include in your Bid. Select the Tax option if you want to apply Tax to the Materials in your Bid.

    • Show These Totals for Doors & Drawers

      • Door Price - Pulled from the Door Catalog. Select the Tax option if you want to apply Tax to the Doors in your Bid.

      • Drawer Front Price - Pulled from the Door Catalog. Select the Tax option if you want to apply Tax to the Drawer Fronts in your Bid.

      • Drawers - Enter an amount per Drawer Box to charge. Select the Tax option if you want to apply Tax to the Drawer Boxes in your Bid.

      • Roll Outs - Enter an amount per Roll Out to charge. Select the Tax option if you want to apply Tax to the Roll Outs in your Bid.

Length Totals

There are three main areas on the Length Totals Tab:

    • Show The Total For Your Cabinets - Select which Cabinet Classes you would like to charge By Lineal Unit and the value. Select the Tax option if you want to apply Tax to the Cabinets in your Bid.

    • Show The Counter Top Totals - If you want to Price your Counter Tops simply choose how you want to Price them (Square Unit or Lineal Unit) and give the value per Unit. Select the Tax option if you want to apply Tax to the Counter Tops in your Bid.

    • Show These Molding Totals - If you wan to Price Crown Molding and/or Scribe simply activate them and enter a value. Select the Tax option if you want to apply Tax to the Moldings in your Bid.

Header

Simply choose what Job data you want displayed in the Reports Header. You can also enter any other specific text you want displayed in the Header.

Footer

Enter your Tax Rate and any other text you want displayed in the Footer.

Extra Line Items

This area allows you to enter any Extra charges you need to add to the Bid. You can add three Taxable and three Non-Taxable line items.
