Catalogs

This sidebar button changes the Bid Center to Catalog Mode. Catalog Mode is primarily used for Pricing with Modular Catalogs and will report pricing information by Catalog. See the Catalog Editor Help file for more information on Modular Catalogs.

The Catalog Information screen will appear. Note the "Catalogs" list on the sidebar. The Catalog that is selected in this list is the active Catalog and this Catalog governs what is displayed and/or available on the screen.

Job Level Catalog Options Window

When the "Generic.cvc" Catalog is highlighted, for example, the Job Level options selected in the Room from the Generic Catalog are displayed in the active Catalog Information Screen. When selected in the "Catalogs" list, all Stock Catalogs will display the Job Level options (for the selected Catalog) as they are used in the Job and the cumulative costs associated to them.

Modular Catalog Word Forms

In addition, all available Microsoft Word Form Templates for the selected Catalog will appear at the top of the Catalog Information Screen in the Report drop down.

These Microsoft Word Form Templates are used to merge Catalog information from Solid Design into printable Microsoft Word documents. These Forms will list all the units in the Room, the Door Styles, and Catalog options used, and the associated prices in a format designed for reporting and ordering purposes.

To activate the Form, click the drop-down arrow next to the Form drop down menu and select the desired Form from the list. Then, click on the "Order Form" option to the right of the drop-down list. When you activate your first Form you will, more than likely, be prompted to browse to the Microsoft Word (WinWord.exe) application. By default Microsoft Word is installed at C:\Program Files\Microsoft Office\Officenn\. nn representing the Version of Office you have installed.

Microsoft Word will open and a security prompt will appear; select "Enable Macros" or "Enable Content" (depending on the Version of Word you are using) on this prompt to proceed.

In versions of Word prior to Word 2007 simply selecting Enable Macros will enable all Word Forms. In Word 2007 and higher the Enable Content option is only per instance. You will need to add the location of the Forms to the Trusted Locations area of the Word Trust Center. To do this follow these steps:

In Word 2007 click the Office button and select Word Options.

In Word 2010 click the File Tab and select Options (highlighted below).

In the Word Options area select Trust Center from the sidebar then click the Trust Center Settings... button.

In the Trust Center select the Trusted Locations option in the sidebar and click the Add New Location.

Click the Browse button.

Browse to the location of the Forms. This is typically C:\Cabinet Vision\Solid_12_0\Database and click "OK".

Close Word and re-open the Form from Cabinet Vision.

Next, an Order Options window will appear prompting the entry of User-defined fields or to pull the requested information from Cabinet Vision (information is pulled from the Job Properties fields). Once this information is satisfactorily entered, click the "OK" button to merge the current Job's Modular Catalog information to the Microsoft Word Form Template.
