Ceilings -

Clicking the Ceilings sidebar button will put you in Ceiling Creation/Editing mode and will bring up two new sidebar buttons:

AutoBuild Ceilings

Click this option to have CABINET VISION automatically build a Ceiling for you. CABINET VISION will evaluate your overall Room size and Build a Ceiling sized for it. You can also use this option to rebuild a Ceiling you have modified back to the default.

New Ceiling

This button allows you to manually add Ceilings to your Room. This option opens The Shape Editor options in the ribbonbar.
