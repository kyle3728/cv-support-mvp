Floors -

Clicking the Floors sidebar button will put you in Floor Creation/Editing mode and will bring up two new sidebar buttons:

AutoBuild Floors

Click this option to have CABINET VISION automatically build a Floor for you. CABINET VISION will evaluate your overall Room size and Build a Floor sized for it. You can also use this option to rebuild a Floor you have modified back to the default.

New Floor

This button allows you to manually add Floors to your Room. This option opens The Shape Editor options in the ribbonbar.
