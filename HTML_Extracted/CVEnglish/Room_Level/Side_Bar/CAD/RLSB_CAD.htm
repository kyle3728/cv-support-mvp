CAD -

The CAD feature is available at the Room Level, Assembly Level, Part Level, and Drawings View by clicking the CAD button on the sidebar. The features of CAD are the same regardless of where (Room Level, Assembly Level, Part Level, or Drawings View) it is selected.

In 3D View the CAD button is disabled until you Save a Camera View. You can apply CAD to any Saved 3D Camera View.

In a Room Level Cross Section View the CAD button is disabled until you Save a Section View. You can apply CAD to any Saved Section View.

See the Main Tab and Right Click Options topics for more information.
