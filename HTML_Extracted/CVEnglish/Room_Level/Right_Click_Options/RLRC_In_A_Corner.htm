Right Click In A Corner -

Solid has the capability of allowing you to very easily create Extended/Return Corner situations based on your choices here. From within a Job place two Cabinets in a Corner leaving a void equal to the Cabinets Depth as demonstrated in the image below:

Once you have established this "void" Right Click in it. And you will receive the Right Click In a Corner menu.

Face Frame Assemblies

  

Frameless Assemblies

Following are the possible options:

 Extend Left End - Will make an Extend Left/Return Right Corner scenario based on your Construction Method settings for the End Stile and Unfinished Ends.

 Extend Right End - Will make an Extend Right/Return Left Corner scenario based on your Construction Method settings for the End Stile and Unfinished Ends.

 Make Void - Will return the Corner to a void.

 Miter Corner - Will Miter the Cabinets into the Corner.

 Miter Frame Both - Will Miter the Stiles at the Corner.

 Miter Frame Left End - Will Miter the Left Stile into the Right at the Corner.

 Miter Frame Right End - Will Miter the Right Stile into the Left at the Corner.
