Properties Group

There are two options in the Properties Group:

Job

The Properties available by selecting Job are the same as Job Properties. Any changes made from the Job Properties area will affect the entire Job with the exception of any Rooms or Assemblies that have Overrides that differentiates them from the Job.

  Room

Properties changed from the Room option ONLY affect the current Room and NOT the Job. Changes here will not affect and Assemblies that have Overrides that differentiate them from the Room. If you need to look at and/or remove Room Level Overrides from the Room you can do that on the Overrides Tab here.

Stock/Modular Catalog Properties

If you are utilizing a Stock/Modular Catalog in your Job it's Properties will be selectable by Catalog name from the Room drop down selector.
