About CABINET VISION

At anytime inside CABINET VISION you have access to the About CABINET VISION information by clicking the small ? option at the top right of the screen. This informational window let's you know the exact Version, Revision, and Build of CABINET VISION you are running. It also has information about how to contact Technical Support.

Clicking the License Details button will let you know which Products, Multipliers, and Additions are on your current CV License.
