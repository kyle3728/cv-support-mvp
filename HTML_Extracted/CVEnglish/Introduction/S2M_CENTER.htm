S2M CENTER

For S2M CENTER Help:

  • In Windows 11 click the Windows Start Button then click All apps >scroll to and select the S2M CENTER 2024 Group and select S2M CENTER 2024 Help.

  • In previous versions of Windows go to  Start | S2M CENTER 2024 | Help

OR

  • Open a Job (it can be a new Job with nothing drawn in it) and go to the S2M CENTER by clicking the S2M CENTER tab in the screen menu. When you are in the S2M CENTER, click the ribbonbar Help option then click the Help Topics... option.
