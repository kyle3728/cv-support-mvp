Perform a Backup

How do I back up my CABINET VISION files?

In Windows 10 or 11 click the Start button or press then select All Apps and scroll to the CABINET VISION 2023 Group and run Backup CV 2024 Settings after making any significant changes to your Material Manager, Material Schedules, Construction Methods, etc. in order to have a backup of your standards should you need them.

 

In previous versions of Windows go to Start | All Programs | CABINET VISION 2024
