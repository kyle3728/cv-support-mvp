Material Parameters

To use query these Parameters simply use them on a part utilizing the material an precede them with _M:

Example _M:DZ

Connectors

Fastenlink

_FLPLUNGEDEPTH - Depth of tool at initial plunge before the ramp down

_FLRAMPDEPTH - Depth of tool at bottom of the ramp down

_FLGLUEDEPTH - Depth of tool at top of the glue pocket

_FLFULLDEPTH - Depth of tool at bottom of cut

_FLRAMPSPAN - Span from entry of fastenlink to bottom of ramp

_FLFLATSPAN - Span from entry of fastenlink to start of start glue pocket

_FLGLUESPAN - Span from entry of fastenlink to start of end glue pocket

_FLFULLSPAN - Span from entry of fastenlink to its assembled position
