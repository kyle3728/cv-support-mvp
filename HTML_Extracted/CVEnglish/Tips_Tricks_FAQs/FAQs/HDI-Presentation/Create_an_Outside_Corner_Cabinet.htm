Create an Outside Corner Cabinet

Ever want to make a corner Cabinet that wraps around the outside of a 90 degree corner?

  1. Place an inverted L Wall.

  1. Position an Upper Cabinet hanging Cabinet Depth past the corner.

  1. Place another Upper Cabinet the same way so it overlaps the first occupying the same space.

  1. Then right click one of the Cabinets, select Combine | Assemblies, then left click the other Cabinet and there you have it!

 

The Cabinets should be combined!
