Show Door Swings on Passage Doors

How can I show Door Swings on Interior/Exterior Doors?

  1. Take a door to the Assembly Level by double clicking it.

  2. From the "Plan" Ortho view, click on "New Part".

  3. From the General Colors part group choose "Black", set it to "Hide in Elevation and Perspective". Click Next.

  4. Set the Length to be the same as the width of the door, set the width and thickness to be 1/16". Click Next.

  5. Set the Orientation to "Top". Click Next through to finish.

  6. Connect the New Part to whichever corner of the Door is going to have the door swing at a 45 degree angle, set the elevation to 0".

  7. After placing the New Part at the angle specified, right click on the New Part and copy it.

  8. Position the copy so it is connected at the other end of the door, angled so it connects at the end of the first New Part.

  9. Highlight the copy, radius it to create an arch. Look at the door from Layout. The lines will not show in 3D or elevation but will show in Floor plan, showing the door swing.

Once you have the door set the way you want, you can save it back into your Catalog.
