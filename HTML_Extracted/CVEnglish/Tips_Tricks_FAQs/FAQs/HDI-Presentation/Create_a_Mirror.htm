Creating a Mirror in CABINET VISION

  1. You have a Mirror Part with a Mirror Material which has a Mirror Finish Type available in the Room Material Schedule.

  2. Draw a wall, place an Empty Upper Object on the Wall, and set the size to be the size of your mirror.

  3. Double click the Blank Object to go to the Assembly Level, Face Ortho view.

  4. Click on New Part, choose the Room Part Group then select the Mirror part for the Part Type, click Next.

  1. Enter the part dimensions to match the size of your mirror (leave thickness alone), click Next.

  2. Choose Front Orientation, click Next.

  3. Leave the Finish options as default (Gray - Very Light).

  4. In the sidebar set the Left/Right position of the Mirror to 0".

  5. Return to Layout level Elevation view, right click on the Mirror, choose Save, Save As...

  6. On the left, choose a Catalog to save the Mirror into.

  7. Give it a name, Mirror would be good.

  8. Click on Preserve Size. Click on OK, to close and save.

You have successfully created and saved a Mirror object into your library.

To view a reflection in the Mirror, choose Architectural Render option from the xRender Tab.

You can edit the shape of this object, add Molding to it, use it for a ceiling tile, place it inside of cabinets, etc.
