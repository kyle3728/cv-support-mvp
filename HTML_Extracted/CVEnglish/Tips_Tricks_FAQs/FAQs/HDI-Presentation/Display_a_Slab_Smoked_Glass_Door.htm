Display a Slab Smoked Glass Door

How can I display a slab type smoked glass Door that is semi transparent?

  1. Open CABINET VISION.

  2. Open the Material Manager.

  1. Click the Finish option in the ribbonbar.

  1. In Finishes click the New tool in the toolbar.

  2. Type Smoked Glass Dark for the Name and Description.

  3. Make sure all the color sliders are to the top and you have a black square showing.

  1. Close the Finishes window and click Yes to save your new Finish.

  2. Click the Finish Type option in the ribbonbar.

  3. Select the Glass Finish Type and then click the Copy tool in the toolbar.

  4. Name your copy Smoked Glass.

  5. Set your Shininess and Transparency similar to the illustration below. You may also want to use the Preview pane to see the new Finish Type applied to your new Finish.

  1. Close the Finish Types window and click Yes to save your new Finish Type.

  2. Select the appropriate Category and select a Miscellaneous Material you are not using and name it Smoked Glass Dark.

  3. With your Smoked Glass Dark Material selected go to the Finishes Tab.

  4. Select the Smoked Glass Dark Finish for the Front, Back, Edge, and End.

  5. Go to the Finish Types tab and select Smoked Glass for the Finish Type for the Front, Back, Edge, and End.

  6. Go to the Textures tab and make sure that Blank is selected for the Front, Back, Edge, and End.

  7. Close the Material Manager.

  8. Open Material Schedules and go to Doors Material Schedules.

  9. Select your favorite Material Schedule.

  10. Expand the Panels branch.

  11. Select your Smoked Glass Dark Material from the Material List and drag it to the "Door Glass Panel" Part.

  12. Close the Material Schedule Manager.

You now have a way to create many different styles of glass and incorporate them into your Assemblies as a solid/transparent Door panel. By experimenting you can also create many new applications to use Finishes with.
