How Do I Add A Cutout to a Part?

  1. Take the Assembly to the Assembly Level.

  2. Go into an Ortho Plan View (or any other Ortho View) and right click on the Top (or whatever Part you want to add a cut-out to) and select "Edit".

  3. Go to the Reports View and click the New Operation button in the sidebar.

  4. Add a "Route" the overall size you want.

  5. After adding the Route, still at the Part Level go to the Reprts view .

  6. Make sure you select the Face in the sidebar that you placed the Operation on.

  7. Right click Edit or Double Click the Route.

You then have the ability to give the route any shape you would like.
