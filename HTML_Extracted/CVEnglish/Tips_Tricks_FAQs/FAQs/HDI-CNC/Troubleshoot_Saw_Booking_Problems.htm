Troubleshoot Saw Booking Problems

or  + 

In the Machine Catalog I have a Max Booking Height of 60mm. Which means my 3/4" material can only stack three tall. I then enter 3 as my "Booking Max Sheets". However, I could stack my 1/4" material ten tall but Solid limits ALL the stacking to three tall. Can I get ten tall for the 1/4" and three tall for the 3/4"?

You should change your Max sheets to 10 and leave the 60mm booking height alone. The S2M CENTER will do the math to calculate how many sheets to stack.
