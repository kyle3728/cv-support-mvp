Remove Toe Kick from Cut List but Not Graphically

I do not want Front Toe on my cut list, however, I do want the toe to display on my elevation and perspective views. How would I go about accomplishing this?

You could write a UCS similar to:

;Cancels out Toe Dimension

For Each FT Part

  EDX = -DX

The EDX variable is added to the DX value when the part is sent to the cut list. It is a way of having the size on the cut list different than the graphic. In this case the EDX will cancel out the DX leaving a result of 0. If either, DX, DY, or DZ of a part is 0 then the part is not output to the cut list.

This method will not function for Parts whose Material includes a Model, Models can not be re-sized.

OR

You could create a Miscellaneous Material in the Material Manager and give it the correct thickness and call it "Front Toe". Apply this Material to your Toe Parts in your Material Schedules. Then it has a length but you will not see it on your cut list.
