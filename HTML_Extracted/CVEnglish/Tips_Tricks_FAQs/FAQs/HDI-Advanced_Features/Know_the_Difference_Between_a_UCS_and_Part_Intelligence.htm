Know the Difference Between a UCS and Part Intelligence

Is there a difference between writing a UCS and giving a part intelligence?

Part intelligence is instructions written at the Part level of the Assembly affecting only that Assembly, while a UCS can be applied to all Assemblies, only specific Assemblies, or all Assemblies meeting specific conditions which you establish in the UCS. There are benefits to both methods; the choice of one over the other requires a comprehensive understanding of how both methods work.
