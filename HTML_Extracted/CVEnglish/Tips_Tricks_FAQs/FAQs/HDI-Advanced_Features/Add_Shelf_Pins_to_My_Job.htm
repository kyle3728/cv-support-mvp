Add Shelf Pins to My Job

I want to add shelf pins to all of my adjustable shelves and have them show up on my Assembly Sheets and Material Summaries. How do I do this?

  1. From the CABINET VISION System Level go to the Part Manager.

  2. Click on the Pulls group in the sidebar.

  3. Click on the New option in the ribbon and Name it SHFPN.

  4. For Description type in Shelf Pin.

  5. Click Close to leave the Parts Manager.

  6. Go to the Material Manager.

  7. Go to the Pull Category or whatever Category you want to use to store the Shelf Pin.

  8. Create a new Miscellaneous Material and name it Shelf Pin (Or whatever name/part number you use).

  9. Return to the opening screen and go to Material Schedules | Pulls.

  10. Attach the Shelf Pin Material to the Shelf Pin item in the Pulls Schedules you use.

  11. Close back out to the opening screen.

Now you can write a User Created Standard to add the Shelf Pin to all of your Adjustable Shelves. Below is the text to utilize in your UCS:

    ;Links 4 Shelf Pins to Each Adjustable Shelf

 

 For Each AS Part

 

  Dim SHFPN as New Pull

  SHFPN.Visible = False

  SHFPN.DX = .125

  SHFPN.DY = .125

  SHFPN.X = 0

  SHFPN.Y = 0

  SHFPN.Z = 0

  SHFPN.AX = 0

  SHFPN.AY = 0

  SHFPN.AZ = 0

  SHFPN.QTY = 4

  1. Now go to a new Job and Place a Cabinet on a Wall.

When you print your Assembly Sheet for that Cabinet, look in the Hardware Section and you will see your Shelf Pin hardware listed.
