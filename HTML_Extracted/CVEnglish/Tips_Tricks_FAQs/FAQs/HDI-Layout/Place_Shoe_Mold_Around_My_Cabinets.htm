Place Shoe Mold Around My Cabinets

How do I place shoe mold around my Base and Tall Cabinets?

Base Molding can only be applied from the Floor Plan view:

  1. Go to Floor Plan.

  2. Click on the Molding button in the sidebar.

  3. Click on Crown to show the drop down arrow and change the type to Base.

  4. Set your Snap Points to snap to Object Points.

  5. Click on New Molding to place your molding as you need it.
