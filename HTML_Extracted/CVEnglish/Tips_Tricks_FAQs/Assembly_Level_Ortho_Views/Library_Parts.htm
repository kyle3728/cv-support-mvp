Library Parts

At the Orthographic levels you may save Parts to your Part Library or add those Library Parts to your Assemblies.

To save a part to the Part Library:

  1. Right-Click on the desired Part.

  2. Click Save to Library.

 

  1. Select the library from the list on the left where you want to save this Part.

  1. Click OK.

To Add a Part to the Cabinet From the Part Library:

  1. Click the Library Part button in the sidebar.

  1. The Part Library window will appear.  

  1. On the left side, select the Catalog where the desired Part is located. Then click the Part that you want to add and click Next.

  1. In the next window, type in the size that you want to have the Library Part come in at.

  1. Choose the orientation that you want the new Part to have.

  1. Select the desired Finish for the new Part. Click Finish to complete the process of importing the Library Part.

Use the size and position fields in the sidebar to locate the Part properly in the Assembly.
