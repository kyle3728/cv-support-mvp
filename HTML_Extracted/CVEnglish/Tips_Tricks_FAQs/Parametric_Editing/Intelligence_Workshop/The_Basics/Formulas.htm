Formulas

I hope you didn't skip ahead to this section. You really need to understand everything that comes before this or you will be lost. Writing a formula is actually fairly simple, the two basic steps are:

  1. Figure out what it is you are trying to do (this is your formula) and write it down (this is the hard part).

  2. Trace it through the Object Tree to get your code (this is the easy part).

Formula Names

Notice how in the above picture, after the names of the Parts, it has letters in brackets; for the "Finished Left End" is says LF, for the "Finished Right End" it says RF, for the "Unfinished Back" it says UB, for the "Deck" it says DE, and for the "Top" it says TO.

These letters in brackets are the names you will be using when you write formulas. In your formula if you wanted to refer to the "Deck", of a Base Cabinet, you would refer to it using "DE" not "Deck". If you wanted to refer to the Top you would use "TO" not "Top".
