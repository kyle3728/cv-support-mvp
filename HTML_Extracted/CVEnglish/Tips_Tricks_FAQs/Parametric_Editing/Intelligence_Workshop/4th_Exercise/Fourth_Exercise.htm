Fourth Exercise

This time we will add a board in the upper right hand opening.  It will run from the bottom of board@1 to the top of board@3.  It will be centered in the opening left to right.

  1. Click on the "Face" ortho view tab, and then click on the "New Part" button.  When the "add a new part" screen comes up, under the "Part Type" area select "Board".

  2. Click on "Next".  This will bring you to the "Size" tab.

  3. If you where to measure the distance from the bottom of BOARD@1 to the top of BOARD@3 it would be 10 3/8".  The length of board@4 is equal to the length of board@2 divided by two minus the width of board@3 divided by two.  Or the length of board@4 is equal to the length of board@2 minus the width of board@3 then the remainder divided by two.  So set the length to 10 3/8", set the "Width" to 4".  Leave the thickness alone.  Click on "Next".

  4. On the "Orientation" tab select "Front" orientation.  Click on "Next".

  5. You should now be at the "Finish" tab.  For this demonstration the finish doesn't matter.  Click on the "Finish" button.

  6. We now need to place the part "Left/Right" in the cabinet.  If your cabinet is still 36" wide, and you have done everything you were supposed to up till now, the "Left/Right" would be 25 5/8".  The X for board@4 is equal to the left and right of BOARD@3 + 1/2 the Length of BOARD@3 - 1/2 the width of BOARD@4.

  7. We now need to place the part "Up/Down" if your cabinet is still 34 1/2" high and you have done everything you were supposed to up till now the "Up/Down" will be 19 3/8".  The up and down of Board@4 is the same as the up and down of BOARD@3.

  8. It now needs to move to the front of the cabinet (24 - .75 = 23.25), so we enter 23 1/4" were it says "In/Out" and press enter.

The part is now rotated and positioned correctly and we are ready to give the part intelligence.
