Creating Glass Doors

You do a glass Door pretty much the same way you build any other Door. Just follow the steps for the type of Door you are trying to make. The only difference is that when you get to the step where you pick the panel type select "Glass" as the Panel Type.

Many times when doing glass Doors the Mid Rails and/or Stiles will be thinner (less in width) than you might normally make them. If this is the case, then you would go to Catalog Properties and change your default Rail and/or Stile widths to suit the Door you are creating (it is easier to create the door if you preset them).
