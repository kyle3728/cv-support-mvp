Shaping the Top or Bottom Rail

To shape the Top or Bottom Rail.

  1. Make sure the Rail is the correct size.

  2. In the Door Editor (Section) right click on the Rail and select "Edit Shape".

If you only want the picture to look good and you don't care if the parts are right you can just select "Arch". Casebuilder will arch the Door for you. However the Parts might not be correct.

Right clicking on the rail and selecting "Edit Shape" will bring you to the Shape Editor. Your screen should look something like below. See The Shape Editor topic for more information on the Shape Editor tools.
