Drill and Dowel Machine

Use the drop down selector here to select your Drill & Dowel Machine. If you do not have an available machine to select here this means that you have either not created a Drill & Dowel Machine or you have not selected an NC Link that is valid for use as a Drill & Dowel Machine. See the Topic on the Machine Catalog for how to create a Drill & Dowel Machine.

Pre-Band - This instructs the S2M CENTER whether you band your cut to size parts prior to placing them on your Drill and Dowel machine. If you do this set Pre-Band to Yes, this will take into account edge banding thickness in Secondary machining operations, otherwise, leave it at the default of No.
