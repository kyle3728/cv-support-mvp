Master Operations

Allows you to determine the color of Master Operations which will not be machined because they do not match properly.

Annotations

When you right click on the X-Section view and Right Click To Drawing, you will have the option to add a label to the Elevation view.

These labels will be placed in the Elevation view to show where the cross section was taken from. If you want the label to appear in the Elevation view, check the box marked "Show This Cross Section in Elevation View" in the window that appears after you click To Drawing.
