Rotate

To Rotate the view, click and hold the left mouse button in the window. Move the mouse up or down, or left or right. In this mode the Rotation when moving the mouse left to right the object is being rotated about the local Y Axis passing through the Object.

If you press while dragging left to right the object will rotate about the world Y axis running vertically down the screen instead of through the object. This allows you to fin tune your view a bit better giving views that are difficult to do with local Y Axis rotation.

The axis for the up and down movement of the mouse is the Floor (0 Elevation). The axis of the left to right movement is the center of the Room. If you notice, with the Grid option on in Floor Plan view, there are 2 darker blue lines. One line that is horizontal and one that is vertical. Where these lines cross is the center of the Room and the intersection of the 2 lines is the pivot point of the left to right motion with the mouse.

If your Room is not Centered you can have CABINET VISION center it automatically by using the Room | Center Room option.
