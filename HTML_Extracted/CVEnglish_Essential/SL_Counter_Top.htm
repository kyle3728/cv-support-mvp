Counter Top

Following are the available Branches when defining a Counter Top Construction Method:

Materials

Build-Up

Alignment Holes

Joining

 If you do not have the CNC Counter Top Module you will not see the Alignment Holes Branch and the Joining Branch will not show all the details outlined here.

CABINET VISION ships with a Counter Tops Construction Method named Sample. You can set up this Construction Method to meet your needs or you can create a new Construction Method by clicking the New option or by copying an existing one. You are not limited in the number of Counter Top Construction Methods that you may create, so be sure to create Methods for all the types of construction that you will be using.
