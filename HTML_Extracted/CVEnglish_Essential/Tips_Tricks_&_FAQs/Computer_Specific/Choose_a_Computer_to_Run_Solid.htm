What kind of Computer should I purchase to run Solid?

The cost of computers continues to drop while their performance continues to go up. Don't try to save money on your hardware; you will only regret it later. Purchase the newest, fastest, computer system you can afford. Avoid purchasing systems with integrated graphics.

If you are unfamiliar with computer configuration, ask for an industry standard commercial quality computer to use at your business.
