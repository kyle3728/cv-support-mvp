Steps to Creating a Door

  1. Click on "Create A New Door Style".

  2. Name the Door.

  3. Select a Category to store the Door in.

  4. Click on the "Construction" tab and create the Door.

  5. Click on the "Profile" tab and select the Profiles to use with this Door.

  6. Click on the "Material" tab and select the Material Schedules to use with this Door.

You do not need to do anything on the "Associated Door" tab to make a Door usable.

Naming the Door

Naming a Door style is accomplished from the opening screen of the Door Catalog Editor.

Select a Category to Store the Door Under

The Door "Category" is what tab the Door is stored on.  When you click on "Select A Door" and go looking for a Door, your Doors are stored on different tabs.  Such as the "Raised Panel" tab or the "Flat Panel" tab. To change what tab the current Door is stored on. Just click on the drop down called "Category" and pick the category you would like the Door to be stored under.

To move a Door from one tab to another, click on "Select Door Style" and then select the Door you wish to move to a new tab. Then just re-select the category for that Door.
