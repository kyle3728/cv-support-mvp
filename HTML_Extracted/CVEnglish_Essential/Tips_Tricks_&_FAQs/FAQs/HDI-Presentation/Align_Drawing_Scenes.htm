Align Drawing Scenes

Is there a way to get Scenes to line up with other Scenes on a sheet (besides "eye balling it")?

Yes, you can use the Point or Grid Snap settings to "Snap" the Scenes where you want them.

To use Point Snaps, use the Snap Marks option to place points where you need them, then change to “Snap to Point” on your Snap settings statusbar button. Now you can "Snap" your Scenes to the Points you've placed.

To use the Grid Points, right click on the screen to turn your Grid pattern on and to modify the Grid Snap spacing and Grid spacing, then change to “Snap to Grid” on your Snap settings toolbar button. Now you can "Snap" your Scenes based on the Grid space settings you've chosen.
