Change the Elevation of Counter Tops

How do you change the Elevation of a Counter Top?

Assuming there is a Counter Top in the Room now:

  1. Go to the Floor Plan view.

  2. Click on the Tops button in the sidebar.

  3. Right click on the Counter Top you want to modify, from the drop down menu â€“ choose Properties.

  4. At the Counter Top Properties screen you will be able to change the Elevation.

If you left click on the Counter Top you want modify, the Counter Top Editor opens to allow you to edit the shape of the Counter Top. You'll want to start from step 1 again if you accidentally left click the Counter Top to modify its position, etc.
