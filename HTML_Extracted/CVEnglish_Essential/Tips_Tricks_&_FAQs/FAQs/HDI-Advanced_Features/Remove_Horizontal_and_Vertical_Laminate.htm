Remove Horizontal and Vertical Laminate

How do I get rid of Horizontal and Vertical laminate on my Cabinets?

  1. From the Solid Splash Screen go to the Material Manager and make sure your "None" Banding Material is exactly that - no thickness, no texture, and no color. Anything else in that Material should be unchecked too.

  2. Then go to your Assembly Parts Material Schedule and make sure that you have the "None" laminate selected for both Vertical and Horizontal Laminate.
