End Type Annotations

Door/Drawer Front Position Numbers

The Door/Drawer Front positions within an Assembly are numbered logically in columns from left to right and with letters logically in Rows. The Door/Drawer Front Position Labels are also retrievable with the parameter named _LABEL in the Object Tree. These Labels are also in Report.mdb for use in Reports at Doors|Label.
