Available Scenes -

This section displays any views that have been sent "To Drawing" using the right click "To Drawing" option. The list will also indicate the status of each scene. An asterisk next to the scene means something has changed since in the view the Scene was shot from. A check mark next to the scene means that the scene is placed on a Drawing Sheet.

Simply drag a Scene from the list to the Drawings area just like you place an Object on a Wall from the Objects area.

You have two options to size a Scene which has been placed on a Drawing Sheet.

  • Scale - See the Right Click on Scene-Page topic for more information.

  • Grip Sizing - When selecting a Scene on the Drawing Sheet you will get handles at the corners and mid points which you can use to re-size the Scene. Utilizing this method will not produce a standard Scaled scene. However, once you get it around the size you want you can go to Scale and tweak the User Scale that is applied using this method.

Once a Scene is placed in the Drawings Area you can double click the Scene to take it to the CAD area for notation.

There is also a Preview option in this area. There are two choices here:

None - Turns off the Scene Preview when selecting a Scene from the Available Scenes list.

All - Displays a Preview for All selected Scenes.
