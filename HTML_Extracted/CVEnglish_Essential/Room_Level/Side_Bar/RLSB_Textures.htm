Textures -

To change the Texture of a Wall, Floor, Ceiling, Counter Top, or Molding, click on the associated Texture swatch, and Texture Manager will open allowing you to select a Texture from the desired category.

When you render the drawing in Solid Render Mode, the changes will be displayed on screen. Again, the best way to learn how this works is to experiment with it to see what works best for you.
