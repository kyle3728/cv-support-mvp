Ceilings -

Clicking the Ceilings sidebar button will put you in Ceiling Creation/Editing mode and will bring up two new sidebar buttons:

AutoBuild Ceilings

Click this option to have Solid automatically build a Ceiling for you. Solid will evaluate your overall Room size and Build a Ceiling sized for it. You can also use this option to rebuild a Ceiling you have modified back to the default.

New Ceiling

This button allows you to add additional manual Ceilings to your Room. This option brings you into the Shape Editor; simply define the boundary of your Ceiling(s).
