Enhance

This option will apply settings that can enhance your red cube Renderings by smoothing the jagged lines typically associated with low resolutions or odd angles. Smoothing has the greatest effect on the render quality. There are several settings in 3D View Properties which allow you to control what Enhance View does.

This option does not affect PhotoVision Renders.
