/ Hide/Unhide Scenes

This option will toggle to hide or unhide all scenes on the current sheet and if these same scenes are used on other Sheets they will be hidden there as well.

To unhide a Scene after it has been hidden, simply right click in the middle of that scene and click Properties.

In the Properties Window that appears, disable the box marked "Hide Scene" then click OK. Â This will cause the Scene to become visible.
