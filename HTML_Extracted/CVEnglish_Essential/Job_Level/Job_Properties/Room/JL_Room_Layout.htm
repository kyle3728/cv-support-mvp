Room Layout

Click on the Categories or Tabs below for more information

Walls

Wall Thickness is the measured thickness of the Wall at the job-site.

Wall Height is the measured height of the Wall at the job-site.

Soffit Height references the distance between the ceiling and the top of an Upper or Tall cabinet. It is measured from the ceiling down. This option does not place a Soffit object in your Room it simply sets a placement value.

Placement

Vertical Positional is the value used if your Catalog Object has been Save with the option Above Vertical Positional or Below Vertical Positional.
