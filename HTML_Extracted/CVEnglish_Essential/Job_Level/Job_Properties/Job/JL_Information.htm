Job Information

Click on the categories or tabs below for more information

Under the Information Tab, you will need to enter information about the Job and the Customer.

Adding the information to this screen is not specifically required in order to create a Job, however it has the advantage of providing information that can be used in templates you may create in the  Bid Center as well as in the Drawings Area.  In those areas, there are variables available which will look to information you have entered here for values.

The Job Number field will be the file name that this Job is saved under. This can be either a name, a number, or a combination of letters and numbers. However, be very careful not to use any punctuation (especially periods) in this field, this can cause the Job to be lost.
