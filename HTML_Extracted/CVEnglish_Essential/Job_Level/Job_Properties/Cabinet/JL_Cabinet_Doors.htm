Cabinet Doors

Click on the categories or tabs below for more information

The Doors Tab is the area of Job Properties where you select the Door Style and Material for the Doors, Drawer Fronts, and End Panels (Panelized Ends and Applied Door) for your Job.

The Door selection interface is designed to allow you to have complete control over the Door Styles for your Cabinets with the fewest selections possible. The most important area in this configuration screen is the All branch. With this you can specify the General Door Style, Material, and Profile the majority of your Doors will be.

For instance, let's say I wanted to have a Cathedral Arch Door for my Upper Cabinets I would simply expand the Upper Branch and select that Door Style and would need to make no other changes. If you will notice in the image below I have selected the Cathedral Arch Raised Panel Door Style for my Upper Cabinets. Also notice that instead of normal text the text is italicized, this is way for you to quickly see that you have selected something that differs from the "All" selection.

Another example would be my Drawer Fronts. I would not do a Square Raised Panel for those but a Solid Wood Drawer Front instead.

You are also not limited to Style differences from the "All" selection as you can see in the Drawer Front example above my Inside Edge Detail and Raised Panel Detail also differ from the "All" selection. I could also choose an alternate Material for my Drawer Fronts as well.

By selecting a Door Style for Base EP (End Panel), Upper EP, and Tall EP you are not necessarily, depending on Finished End type in the Construction Method, changing or modifying your Cabinet Ends, instead, you are selecting the style of Door that will be used only if you do select to have these ends on a Cabinet, Room, or the Job.

To remove all of the Door Overrides (Sub items that differ from the All Parent) simply click the Remove Overrides button.
