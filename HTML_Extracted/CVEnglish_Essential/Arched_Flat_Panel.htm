Creating an Arched Flat Panel Door

To create an Arched Flat Panel Door with cope & stick construction you must first have an "Inside Edge Profile" that matches your door cutters. See "Checking Door Stile Sizes", "Inside Edge Profile" and "Checking Door Rail Sizes".

  1. Click on the "Create A New Door Style" tool.

  2. Name the Door.

  3. Select a Category to store the Door on, use the "Flat Panel" category.

  4. Click on the "Construction" tab.

  5. Click in the middle of the door select "Flat Panel" as the panel type for this Door.

  6. Select a sample "Inside Edge Profile" to use with this Door.

  7. Select a sample "Raised Panel Detail" to use with this Door.

  8. Click the "Add Top and Bottom Rail, Left and Right Stile" tool.

  9. Click on the Top Rail and make the Rail wider by the amount of the arch.

  10. Right click on the Top Rail and select "Custom Shape".

  11. Shape the Top Rail to your specifications.

  12. Check the Door part sizes.

  13. Click on the "Profiles" tab and select the appropriate Profiles to use with this Door.

  14. Click on the "Materials" tab and select the appropriate Material Schedules to use with this Door.
