Graph

The Graph sidebar button changes the Bid Center to Graph Mode. The Graph button is only available when in Internal Mode. Graphs split your current Bid into three Parts:

Cost - The Cost item represents your Cost as defined by the active Rate Table(s).

Tax - The Tax item represents the Tax on the Job.

Profit - The Profit item represents your Profit on the Job as defined by the Markup used for the Job.

Graph Side Bar Options

There are several options in the sidebar to alter the look and feel of the Graph that is displayed in the Main Screen.

Main Title

This is where you define the Title of the displayed Graph. The area where this is displayed is highlighted in the following image.

Bottom Title

Style

There are five different Graph Styles to choose from as well as the option to display the Graph Flat or in 3D. The five styles are:

  • Vertical Bars

  • Horizontal Bars

  • Pie

  • Pie Data Labels

  • Pie Percent Labels

Information

There are two options in the Information section:

  • Labels - Allows you to suppress the display of Labels on your Graph.

  • Legends - Allows you to suppress the display of Legends on your Graph.

Grid

There are two options in the Grid section:

  • Vertical Lines - Allows you to display Vertical Grids on your Bar Graphs.

  • Horizontal Lines - Allows you to display Horizontal Grids on your Bar Graphs.
