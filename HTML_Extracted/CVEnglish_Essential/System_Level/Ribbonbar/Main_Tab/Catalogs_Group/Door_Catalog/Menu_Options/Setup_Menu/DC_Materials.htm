Materials

This is simply another place that you can go to create and change Material Schedules for Doors. This window works in exactly the same manner as the primary Door Material Schedule Editor.

You will notice in the sidebar there are Laminate and Banding options. What you do is select a Material Schedule to work on by clicking on the drop down list. You then turn on and off the laminate and banding options by enabling or disabling the options you want. For example, in the window above I have my "Alder" Material Schedule picked and none of the "Laminate Options" are enabled. So when I use the "Alder" Material Schedule for a Door it will not laminate the face, laminate the back, or band the edges. In the following picture I have selected my "Alder Band" Material Schedule, you will note that the "Band Edges" option has been enabled. So when I use this Material Schedule for a Door the Door will be banded with whatever I selected for "Door Edge Banding" when I setup the "Alder Band" Material Schedule.

In the following picture I have selected my "Solid Laminate" Material Schedule. You will note that the "Laminate Face", "Laminate Back", and the "Band Edges" options have all been checked.  So when I use this Material Schedule for a Door it will laminate the face of the Door, the back of the Door, and band the edges of the Door with whatever I selected for "Door Face Laminate", "Door Back Laminate", and "Door Edge Banding", when I setup the "Solid Laminate" Material Schedule.  I will have to be careful which Doors I use this Schedule for, as it will laminate the face and band the edges no matter what kind of Door it is.
