Door Catalog - Right Click A Rail

There are four options when Right Clicking on a Top, Bottom, or Mid Rail.

Delete

This menu option will delete the selected Stile or Rail.

Square

This menu option will remove any Shaping from the selected Rail and make it Square.

Arch

This menu option will add an Arch to the selected Rail

Custom Shape...

It is possible to custom edit your Door Rails. Only one edge of Top and Bottom Rails may be edited, and it must be an Inside Edge, both edges of Door Mid Rails may be edited.
