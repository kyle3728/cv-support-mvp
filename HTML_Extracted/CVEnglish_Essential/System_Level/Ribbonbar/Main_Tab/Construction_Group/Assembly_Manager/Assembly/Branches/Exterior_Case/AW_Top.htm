Top

This is the top of the Cabinet carcass, not to be confused with the Counter Top. The Top of a Cabinet may be a full Top, Stretchers, a Web Frame, or Gussets (corner blocks). You may also choose to build some Cabinets with no Tops.

Top Finished Side Orientation (Standard #125)

In the Construction Method, you have the ability to determine the Material face orientation if you are using 2 sided Materials for the Top of a Cabinet. You can select whether the "good side" faces in or out (up or down on the Top). Remember to add break outs if needed for the different Classes of Assemblies.

Top Grain Alignment (Standard #126)

For Tops in a Corner sub-branch, you have the ability to specify Grain Alignment for Tops on 45 Corner Cabinets just like with Shelves.

Gusset Size (Standard #149)

An alternative Top type that is available is the option for Gussets. These are triangular corner blocks usually used for Base Cabinets that add rigidity with less Material usage.
