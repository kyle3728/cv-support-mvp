The Camera button, available in the sidebar, will open the Camera Editor giving you the ability to set a view position to show a perspective view of your design. The closer the Camera position is to what you are viewing the more intense the perspective.

Simply drag the Camera around to where you want to view from, you can also drag the view point end and move, lengthen, or shorten it. Following are the options available in the Camera Editor sidebar:

  • Position - Height - The Height of the Camera from the Floor.

  • Position - Target Height - The Height of the view point's end.

  • Lens - Angle - The Angle width of the Camera Lens.

The Preview button will allow you to Preview the results in the View without having to Close the Camera Editor.
