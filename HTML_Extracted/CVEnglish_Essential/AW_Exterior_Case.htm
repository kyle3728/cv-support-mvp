Exterior Case Branch

These Parts are part of the Exterior Case branch. Typically, these are visible Parts, yet visibility is not a requirement for inclusion in this group. When answering the questions for Tops, you must choose between a Stretcher, no Top, or a solid Top. It may be that you desire Stretchers on your Base Cabinet, yet you need a solid Top for your Wall and Tall Cabinets. This is a classic example of the need to add a break out for the different Cabinet Classes.
