About Casebuilder

At anytime inside Casebuilder you have access to the About Casebuilder information by clicking the small ? option at the top right of the screen. This informational window let's you know the exact Version, Revision, and Build of Casebuilder you are running. It also has information about how to contact Technical Support.

Clicking the License Details button will let you know which Products and Add-Ons are on your current Casebuilder License.
