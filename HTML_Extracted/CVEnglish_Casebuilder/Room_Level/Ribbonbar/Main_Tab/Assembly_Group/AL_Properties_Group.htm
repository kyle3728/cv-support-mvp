Assembly Properties

Always remember, the level at which you make a change will limit the scope or reach of that change. Since these changes are made at the Assembly Level, they will be limited to the Assembly you are editing. This allows the option to use the "Construction" or "Material" tab to change the Construction or Materials of just this Assembly. The logic is the same for the other Properties available here. This would be the proper method for changing an Assembly in an Room to be of a different Material than the other Assemblies.
