Right Click Standard Interior Splits Section View - Section Interior

Standard Interior Splits consist of:

  • Shelf

  • Adjustable Shelf

  • Glass Adj Shelf

  • Fixed Shelf

  • Drawer Stretcher

  • Top

  • Bottom

  • Partition

 

There is one option when right clicking on the Parts listed above in the Section Case view:

  • Delete - This option allows you to delete the selected Part.
