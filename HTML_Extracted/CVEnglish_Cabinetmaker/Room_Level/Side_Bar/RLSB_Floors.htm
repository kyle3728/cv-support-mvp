Floors -

Clicking the Floors sidebar button will put you in Floor Creation/Editing mode and will bring up two new sidebar buttons:

AutoBuild Floors

Click this option to have Cabinetmaker automatically build a Floor for you. Cabinetmaker will evaluate your overall Room size and Build a Floor sized for it. You can also use this option to rebuild a Floor you have modified back to the default.

New Floor

This button allows you to add additional manual Floors to your Room. This option brings you into the Shape Editor; simply define the boundary of your Floor(s).

 

See the Right Click on a Floor topic for more information on editing your Floors.
