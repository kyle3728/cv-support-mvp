Leica DXF Import

Both the Leica S910 and the 3D DISTO will create DXF files as an option to the direct link with Cabinet Vision. The DXF is very handy when you already have the Room designed and want to verify field dimensions against your existing design.

Simply browse to and Open the DXF.

Then confirm the Unit of Measure used for the Capture.

These import point cloud are visible in Plan, Elevation, and 3D Views and their visibility is controlled by the Leica Layer.
