Door

Every Door or Drawer Front must have at least one Material Schedule assigned to it. Door and Drawer Front construction is set up in the Door Manager. Also, you will assign Material Schedules to your Door from the Door Manager.

Sidebar/Properties

The following Properties are available for Doors:

  • ID - The ID number for the selected Material Schedule.

  • Name - The Name of the selected Material Schedule.

  • Description - The Description of the selected Material Schedule.

  • Buy Out -  Enable this option you Buy-Out the Door rather than building it yourself. This will tie in to your Reports and Bids.

  • Cost/Door - This option will simply add the entered amount as a flat rate per Door.

  • Cost/SqFt (SqM) - This option will multiply the entered amount by the total area of the Door.

  • Minimum Cost - This option will allow you to set a minimum Door cost for the Door when using this Material Schedule. Note that total door cost is Door + Mat + Profile (Inside Edge + Outside Edge + Raised Panel + Custom Route). This value is only for the Material cost so if the Material "Add Per Door" and the Material "Add Per SqFt" is less than "Min Cost" it will use Min Cost for the Material cost. In other words... the Door Cost and Profile Costs will be added to this Minimum Cost.

  • Banding - Instructs Cabinetmaker to apply the Material assigned to the Door Edge Banding Part to your Door.

Because you control the Laminate and Banding through the Door and Drawer Front Material Schedules, you may wish to create duplicate Schedules with one having Laminate and Banding active and another without. Remember to name the Schedules in a way that lets you know that Laminate is turned on. For example, you may want to have a Schedule called Birch and one called Birch Band. This will allow you to know by name that one Material Schedule is for a Birch Door that has banding turned on.

Unlike Material Schedules for Assembly Parts, the Door Material Schedules allow you to turn Banding on or off in the Material Schedule itself. If the Material Schedule that you are creating will be used on Doors that you want to edgeband, simply enable the Banding property.
