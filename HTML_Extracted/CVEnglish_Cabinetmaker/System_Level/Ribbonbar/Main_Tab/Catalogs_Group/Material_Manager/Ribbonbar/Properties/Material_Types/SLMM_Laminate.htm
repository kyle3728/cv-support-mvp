Laminate

This topic covers all the Properties for the Laminate Material Type.

Material Group

Previously covered in the Banding Material Type topic.

Laminate Group

  • Oversize - Laminate will be oversized by this amount so that it can be trimmed to fit the Part more exactly. For the example below, edge trim is set to 1 inch.

Size Group

  • Width - The Width of the Material.

  • Length - The Length of the Material.

  • Thickness - The Thickness of the Material. The Thickness is the most important property of a Material because the construction of your Cabinets will depend on the Material Thickness in order to give you a correct cut list.
