Adjustable Shelf

An Adjustable Shelf is one that can be placed in different positions within a Cabinet or Closet. You will define the clearance in front and to the ends of this Adjustable Shelf, and these dimensions will be subtracted from the Assembly's interior dimension to arrive at the Adjustable Shelf dimension.
