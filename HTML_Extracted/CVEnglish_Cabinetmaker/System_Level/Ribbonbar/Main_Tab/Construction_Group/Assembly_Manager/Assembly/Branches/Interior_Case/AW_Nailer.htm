Nailer

Whether you have a top nailer, mid nailer, or a bottom nailer is set here.  Whether you choose to have them outside or inside of your back is also set. The width of a nailer is entered in number format.

The following diagram shows the options available in the Nailer Branch.

Use Mid Nailer (Standard #202)

If you want a Mid Nailer in your Assemblies simply select and establish the Minimum Height of the Assembly. Once the Assembly Height you establish is exceeded the Mid Nailer will appear.
