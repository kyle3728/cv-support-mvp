Fixed Shelf Branch

A Fixed Shelf is yet another horizontal part that is permanently mounted within a Cabinet. A Fixed Shelf is sometimes called a Horizontal Partition. There is no option to adjust the setback of the Fixed Shelf front, as it is flush with either the back of the Face Frame or the Case, depending on the Construction Style. Typically, a Fixed Shelf divides a Tall Cabinet.  Face Frame Cabinets typically have Mid Rails in front of Fixed Shelves, while Frameless Cabinets split Doors or Doors and Drawers at the Fixed Shelf.
