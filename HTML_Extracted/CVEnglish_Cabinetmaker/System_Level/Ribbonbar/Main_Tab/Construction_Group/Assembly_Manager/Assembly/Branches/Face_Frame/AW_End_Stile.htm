This Construction Method Branch is also where you configure the setup of Extended and Return Corner conditions.

Cabinetmaker has the capability of allowing you to very easily create Extended/Return Corner situations based on your choices here and in the Unfinished End Wizard. See the Right Click In A Corner topic for more information.
