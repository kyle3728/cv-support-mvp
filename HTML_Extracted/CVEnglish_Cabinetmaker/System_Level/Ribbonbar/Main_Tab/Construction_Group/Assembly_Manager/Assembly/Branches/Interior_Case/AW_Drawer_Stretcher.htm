Drawer Stretcher

Drawer stretchers, sometimes called spreaders, separate Drawers on Cabinets. You will provide a numeric value for their width. Other questions establish whether you have one at the top Drawer only, or multiple Drawer Stretchers.

 Regardless of how you answer these questions, you will not see the Drawer Stretcher in a Cabinet until you edit a Cabinet and place one there, and save the modified Cabinet back to your library.
