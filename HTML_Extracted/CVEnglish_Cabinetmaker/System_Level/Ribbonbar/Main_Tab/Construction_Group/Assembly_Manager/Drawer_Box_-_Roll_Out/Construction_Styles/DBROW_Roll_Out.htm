General

The following diagram shows the options available in the General object.

Roll Out Height Specifications (#003)

Because the Height of the Roll Out cannot be controlled by the opening size, there is only one box to be filled out for the Height of the Roll Out. Enter the most common height for a Roll Out in this area. Then, if a specific Roll Out needs to be a different Height, it can be changed in the Section Editor.

Roll Out Front

The following diagram shows the options available in the Roll Out Front object.

Roll Out Front Banding Definition (#153)

Roll Out Front Laminate (#036)

Specify here whether you Laminate your Roll-Out Front or not.

Roll Out Swoop Shape (#037)

You have two options for the Swoop option:

  • No - Use this option if you do not Swoop your Roll Out Front.

  • Yes - Use this option if you do Swoop your Roll Out Front. Once this option is selected you will see the Edit button, click this button to control the shape of your Roll Out Swoop.

Swoop Part Shaper

See the Shape Editor Topic for more information.

Roll Out Front Scribe (#038)

You have two options for the Front Scribe:

  • Touches Box Front

  • Space - A positive number represents how far in your want the Roll Out Front to be from the front of the Box. A negative number represents how far outside the front of the Box you want the Roll Out Front to be located.

Roll Out Front - Roll Out Bottom Joint (#039)

You have two options for the Front - Bottom Joint:

  • To Top of Roll Out Bottom (Dado) - This option places the Roll Out Front on top of the Roll Out Bottom. A positive adjustment indicates a Dado into the Bottom a negative adjustment pulls the Roll Out Front up from the Bottom.

  • To Bottom of Roll Out Bottom (Adjustment) - This option places the Roll Out Front flush with the Roll Out  Bottom's bottom. A positive adjustment pushes the Roll Out Front past the bottom of the Bottom. A negative adjustment pulls the Roll Out Front up from the bottom of the Bottom.

Roll Out Front - Roll Out Side Joint (#040)

You have three options for the Roll Out Front - Side Joint:

  • To Roll Out Side (Dado) - A value of 0 would indicate a butt joint, a positive value would indicate a Dado Depth, a negative value would pull the Roll Out Front away from the Roll Out Side.

  • To Outside of Roll Out Side (Adjustment)

Roll Out Front Top Reference (#041)

You have two options for the Roll Out Front Top Reference:

  • Flush to Roll Out Top

  • Space - A positive value adjusts the Roll Out Front below the top of the Box. A Negative value adjusts the Roll Out Front above the top of the Roll Out.
