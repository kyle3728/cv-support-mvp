Change the Elevation of Moldings

How do you change the Elevation of Moldings?

Assuming there is molding in the Room now:

  1. Go to the Floor Plan view (or whichever view you were in when you added the molding).

  2. Click on the Molding button in the sidebar.

  3. Change to the correct Molding Type. (If you applied Base Board Molding, it will not show unless you change to Base Board Type.)

  4. Right click on the Molding you want to modify, from the drop down menu – choose Properties. (You may need to zoom in if your molding is thin and hard to right click on.)

  5. At the Molding Properties screen you will be able to change Material, Profile, Description, Elevation, and Mirror/Rotate the Profile.

If you left click on the molding you want modify, the Molding Editor opens to allow you to edit the shape of the Molding. You'll want to start from step 1 again if you accidentally left click the Molding to modify its position, etc.
