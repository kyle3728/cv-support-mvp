Creating a Arched Raised Panel Door

To create an Arched Raised Panel Door with cope & stick construction you must first have an "Inside Edge Profile" that matches your door cutters.

  1. Select a Category to store the Door on, use the "Raised Panel" category.

  2. Click on the New option in the ribbon.

  3. Name and give a Description for the Door.

  4. Select Multi Piece for the Type.

  5. Click Next.

  6. Select Raised for the Panel Type.

  7. Click Finish.

  8. Double click the Door to go to Door Properties.

  9. Click Section in the ribbon.

  10. Click on the Top Rail and make the Rail the width you need for the arch.

  11. Right click on the Top Rail and select "Arch" or "Edit Shape" to shape the Top Rail to your specifications.

  12. Click Part List in the ribbon to check the Door part sizes.
