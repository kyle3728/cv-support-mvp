About Cabinetmaker

At anytime inside Cabinetmaker you have access to the About Cabinetmaker information by clicking the small ? option at the top right of the screen. This informational window let's you know the exact Version, Revision, and Build of Cabinetmaker you are running. It also has information about how to contact Technical Support.

Clicking the License Details button will let you know which Products and Add-Ons are on your current CV License.
