The S2M Optimizer Button

New

+

When you click on New, the S2M Optimizer will start the Job Properties area to allow you to define a new Job.

Open

+

Clicking on Open will bring up a Windows Open window that you may use to browse to and open a PNC Job file. By default, Jobs are stored in the NcCenter_6_0 folder.

To open a PNC file, simply click on the PNC that you want to open and click the Open button.

Export

+

Saves the Open Job.

Properties

There are three tabs available in Properties:

Information

Allows you to enter Job Information that will be used in Reports.

Comment

Allows you to enter Comments for the current Job.

Schedule

Allows you to set Schedules for the current Job.
