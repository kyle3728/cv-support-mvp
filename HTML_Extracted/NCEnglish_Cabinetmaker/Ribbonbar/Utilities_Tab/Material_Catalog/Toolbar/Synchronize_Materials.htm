Synchronize Materials

This tool will allow you to undo changes you may have made to the Properties of Imported Materials within the S2M CENTER.

Simply select the Items and/or Properties you want to Sync with your Design/Manufacturing product.

This is a one way Synchronization. This tool is designed to "Pull" changes in from your Design/Manufacturing product. The Synchronize tool will not "Push" changes from the S2M CENTER to your Design/Manufacturing product.
