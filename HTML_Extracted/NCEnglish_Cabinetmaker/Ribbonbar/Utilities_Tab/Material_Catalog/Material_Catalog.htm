Material Catalog

When first entering the Material Catalog in the S2M Center you will see a screen similar to the one below with no Materials.

The first thing you will want to do is Import your Materials from Cabinetmaker. To do this click the Import Materials tool in the toolbar. Upon entering the Import Materials tool you will be presented with the following screen.

The S2M Center will scan your hard-drive and attempt to locate the Material databases from the supported Design/Manufacturing packages. If it finds them the Icon for the product will be in color.

If the Icon is in color and you have more than one Version of the product click the icon to browse to the Version you wish to import Materials from.

If the S2M Center does not find the database the products icon will be grayed out. If you know the product is installed and want to browse to the Material Database you can do so by clicking on the grayed out product icon. If you would like to browse to an alternative database for your product you can click on the product icon and browse to the alternative data source. Place a check mark to each Material you would like to Import in the list.

Simply click Import once you have checked off those Materials you would like to Import. You should notice that once you click Import each Material you selected is removed from the list. This will keep you from double Importing Materials.

Simply click Close to return to the Material Catalog. You will notice it is now populated with your Materials.
