Zoom Group

The Zoom Group has three options:

Window

The Zoom Window option is used for increasing the size of an area or Part on your screen to see a detail or examine a condition. By clicking on the Zoom Window tool, then clicking in the center of the area you would like to Zoom In to, and, while holding the mouse button down, dragging the cursor diagonally across the item you wish to Zoom In on, finally releasing the mouse button at the end of the area, your screen will Zoom In.

Out

The Zoom Out option isn't a click and drag task, instead, simply clicking the Zoom Out option will incrementally Zoom the drawing out.

All

The Zoom All option will restore the object size to the original size and re-center the object in the process.
