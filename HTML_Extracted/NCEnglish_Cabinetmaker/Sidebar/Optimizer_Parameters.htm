Optimizer Parameters

There are two tabs in the Nesting Optimizer Parameters screen:

Patterns Tab

Following are the main options in the Patterns Tab:

  1. You can control the Quality of your Nest. Moving the slider to the Right improves the results of the Nest.

  2. You can control the Pack direction of your Parts in the nest with this option.
