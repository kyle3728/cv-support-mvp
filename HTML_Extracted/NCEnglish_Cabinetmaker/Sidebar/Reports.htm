Reports

When your CNC Output is complete, the Reports area allows you to print information about your Output.

Ribbonbar

Return

Probably the most used option in the ribbonbar. This option allows you to go back to the previous screen and/or mode of operation.

Keystroke

Zoom Group

In - Will incrementally Zoom In your Report

Out - Will incrementally Zoom Out your Report.

Page - Will Zoom to fit the entire page in the Viewing area.

Page Width - Will Zoom to fit the page's Width in the viewing area.

Navigator

This tool allows you to navigate from Page to Page in the currently visible Report.

Sidebar

Report Group

The Report Group area allows you to view the available Reports.
